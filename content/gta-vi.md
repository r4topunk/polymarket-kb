## 2026-02-24 — GTA VI confirmed for November 19, 2026 release

Rockstar Games officially confirms Grand Theft Auto VI will release November 19, 2026 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. PC version expected later, possibly 2027. Game features first female protagonist (Lucia) and male lead (Jason), set in Vice City and state of Leonida. Expected pricing $80-100, higher than previous entries.

Impact: Resolves Polymarket question "GTA VI released before June 2026?" as NO (releases November, well after June). Market already reflects this at 0.031 YES price (96.9% NO). Release date has been public for some time, market fully priced.

## 2026-02-24 — Take-Two reaffirms November 19 release date, marketing campaign incoming
Take-Two Interactive confirmed GTA 6 remains on track for November 19, 2026 launch. Official marketing campaign to begin summer 2026. Development costs estimated at $10M/month during six-month delay from May to November.
Impact: Reconfirmation of known release date, no change to market expectations. Marketing timeline provides visibility but doesn't affect primary June/November markets.
