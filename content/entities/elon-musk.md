# Elon Musk

Entrepreneur and Tesla/SpaceX founder who joined Trump administration as head of Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE). Served in advisory role to Trump's second term beginning January 2025.

## Key events
- 2026-02-26T20:33 — Congress rejects majority of DOGE spending cut proposals; estimated actual savings ~$20-40B vs DOGE's $2T target. 2026 budget increases overall spending. [Trump Policy](/topics/trump-policy)
- 2026-02-27T12:30 — Musk departs Trump administration after DOGE falls far short of targets. States he would not repeat the experience. DOGE officially ends July 4, 2026 per original EO. Budget battles shift to impoundment disputes in courts. [Trump Policy](/topics/trump-policy)

## Profile
Musk led Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) as special advisor under Trump. Initiative claimed $160B in identified savings but independent analysis shows Congress rejected ~93% of proposed cuts. Actual budget discipline minimal. Departure signals frustration with congressional resistance to deep spending reductions.
