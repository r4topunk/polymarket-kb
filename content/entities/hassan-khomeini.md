# Hassan Khomeini

Grandson of Ayatollah Khomeini (founder of Iranian Revolution, 1979). Emerged as contender in succession race following Feb 28, 2026 death of Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. Less militaristic than Mojtaba Khamenei; considered pragmatist option within clerical establishment. ## Succession scenario
If Ali Khamenei death is confirmed, Assembly of Experts must select new Supreme Leader within days per Article 111 of Iranian Constitution. Hassan Khomeini represents "pragmatist" clerical option vs. IRGC military takeover or Mojtaba Khamenei (family continuity) scenarios. His position as grandson of revolution founder gives legitimacy within clerical hierarchy despite lack of military background.
