# CLARITY Act

Crypto-Friendly crypto legislative bill expected to pass by April 2026 with 80-90% odds per Ripple CEO. Classifies digital assets as Digital Commodities under CFTC jurisdiction (not SEC). Removes securities classification uncertainty. Counterpart to GENIUS Act on stablecoin regulation.
