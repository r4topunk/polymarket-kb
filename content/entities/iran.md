# Iran (Islamic Republic)

Theocratic state in Middle East. Supreme Leader (Khamenei, died Feb 28, 2026) exercises absolute authority over military, judiciary, media. Home to 125,000+ IRGC personnel. Nuclear program (enrichment paused June 2025, resumed late 2025) and ballistic missile program primary US-Israel concern. Targeted in Operation Epic Fury (Feb 28, 2026) with Khamenei killed, IRGC HQ destroyed, succession crisis imminent.
