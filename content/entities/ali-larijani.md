## Regime continuity scenario
If Ali Khamenei death confirmed, Iran's constitution (Article 111) triggers immediate succession via Assembly of Experts and Provisional Leadership Council (President, Chief Justice, Guardian Council cleric). Ali Larijani likely chairs provisional council as senior surviving civilian, with formal successor selected from clerical candidates (including brother Sadiq). His survival during strikes makes him critical node in regime continuity. IRGC military control trajectory depends on Larijani brothers' ability to coordinate clerical establishment.
