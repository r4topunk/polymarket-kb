# Reza Pahlavi

Exiled son of Mohammad Reza Pahlavi (last Shah of Iran, deposed in 1979 revolution). Living in exile, returned to political relevance following Feb 28, 2026 Operation Epic Fury strikes and death of Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. Calling for regime change and military defection. ## Regime change scenario
Reza Pahlavi represents monarchist opposition to Islamic Republic. While restoration of Pahlavi dynasty (1926-1979 pre-revolution) remains extremely unlikely per experts, his renewed visibility reflects Trump administration's explicit regime change objective in Operation Epic Fury. IRGC defections (none confirmed yet) would be prerequisite for either clerical succession (Mojtaba, Hassan Khomeini, Sadiq Larijani) or broader regime transformation including Pahlavi's potential role. Polymarket regime-fall markets pricing low probability of monarchist restoration (~1-2%).
