# DOGE

Department of Government Efficiency — Trump administration initiative led by Elon Musk to identify spending cuts. Officially ends July 4, 2026 per original executive order.

## Key events
- 2026-02-26T20:33 — Congress rejects majority of DOGE spending cut proposals; estimated actual savings $20-40B vs $2T target ([Trump Policy](/topics/trump-policy))
- 2026-02-27T12:30 — Musk departs Trump administration after DOGE falls far short of targets; states would not repeat experience ([Trump Policy](/topics/trump-policy))

## Profile
Claimed $160B in identified savings; independent analysis shows Congress rejected ~93% of proposed cuts. Actual budget discipline minimal. Budget battles shift to impoundment disputes in courts.
