# Axiom (Axiom Exchange)

YC-backed cryptocurrency exchange (Y Combinator W25 batch, $390M revenue). Suffered insider trading scandal exposed by ZachXBT (Feb 26, 2026). Senior business development employee Broox Bauer used internal "God mode" dashboard to access private wallet data and front-run user trades for 10+ months, pocketing $400k+. Axiom acknowledged breach, revoked access. SDNY may investigate under insider trading statutes.
