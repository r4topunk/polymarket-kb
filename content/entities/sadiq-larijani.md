# Sadiq Larijani

Senior Iranian cleric and political figure. Key succession candidate in post-Khamenei regime transition. Brother of Ali Larijani (former nuclear negotiator). ## Succession scenario
If Ali Khamenei death confirmed, Assembly of Experts must select Supreme Leader per Iranian Constitution Article 111. Sadiq Larijani represents clerical establishment continuity option. Brother Ali Larijani (survived Feb 28 strikes) provides emergency civilian leadership while Assembly convenes, with Sadiq as formal successor candidate.
