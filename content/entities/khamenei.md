Ayatollah Ali Khamenei (born April 19, 1939) was killed Feb 28, 2026, age 86, in US-Israeli joint strikes on his Tehran compound during Operation Epic Fury. Served as Supreme Leader of Iran since 1989. His son Mojtaba (age 56) is a leading succession candidate. His killing triggers Iran's constitutional succession process.
