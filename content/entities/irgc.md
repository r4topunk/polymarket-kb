# IRGC (Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps)

Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps — elite military branch reporting directly to Supreme Leader Khamenei. Controls ballistic missile program, oversees nuclear facilities security, commands air defense systems. Estimated 125,000+ personnel; parallel to regular Iranian army. Targeted heavily in Operation Epic Fury (Feb 28, 2026).
