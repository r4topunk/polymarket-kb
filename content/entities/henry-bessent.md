# Henry Bessent

US Treasury Secretary under Trump administration (second term, 2025-). Leading US economic policy and international economic negotiations on Ukraine reconstruction and trade.

## Key events
- 2026-02-26T15:33 — Bessent signs US-Ukraine minerals deal with Ukrainian Deputy PM Svyrydenko, finalizing joint reconstruction investment fund. Deal establishes US mineral access rights in Ukrainian reconstruction; does not include new security guarantees. ## Policy role
Bessent represents Trump administration economic leverage over Ukraine, negotiating reconstruction terms tied to mineral access rather than security guarantees. His signature on the Feb 26 minerals deal signals Treasury-led approach to Ukraine policy, subordinating security guarantees to economic/resource extraction.
