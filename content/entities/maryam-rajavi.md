# Maryam Rajavi

Leader of the National Council of Resistance of Iran (NCRI), an opposition coalition. Advocates for secular, democratic governance. Emerged as potential succession candidate following Khamenei's death.

Related topics: [Khamenei Succession](/topics/khamenei-succession), [Iran Regime Change](/topics/iran-regime-change)

## Profile

- Title: President-elect of NCRI (in exile framework)
- Organization: National Council of Resistance of Iran (NCRI)
- Platform: Dissolve IRGC, separate religion from state, democratic governance, gender equality
