# Kalshi

US-based prediction market platform offering markets on elections, sports, economics, and weather. Operates under CFTC exemptive order for "event contracts" (prediction markets). Primary competitor to Polymarket in US prediction market space.

- 2026-02-27T23:55 — Kalshi fines MrBeast editor $20K for insider trading; opens 200 insider trading probes. [Crypto Insider Trading](/topics/crypto-insider-trading)

## Insider trading enforcement
Kalshi has significantly escalated insider trading enforcement as of late Feb 2026:
- $20K fine on YouTube content creator (MrBeast editor) for trading prediction markets on non-public event knowledge
- 200 separate insider trading investigations opened
- Policy changes pending that could reshape market surveillance across US prediction markets
