# General Mohammad Hossein Mousavi

Iran's Chief of Staff and senior IRGC military strategist. Killed Feb 28, 2026, in US-Israeli Operation Epic Fury strikes on Tehran.

## Profile

- **Role**: Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces (Iran's highest military position)
- **Organization**: IRGC (Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps)
- **Age at death**: Mid-60s
- **Key function**: Strategic military planning and coordination across all Iranian military branches
- **Death date**: Feb 28, 2026, in Operation Epic Fury airstrikes

## Significance

Mousavi was the de facto military chief of Iran's armed forces and one of the top operational planners for Iran's response to US military pressure. His death alongside Supreme Leader Khamenei represents a decapitation strike targeting both political (clerical) and military leadership simultaneously.

## Key Events

- **2026-02-28T20:15** — Operation Epic Fury executed; Mousavi confirmed killed in strikes
- **2026-03-01T03:00** — IRGC command structure severely disrupted with both Khamenei and Mousavi dead
- **2026-03-01T14:20** — NPR confirms: Chief of Staff General Mousavi killed in strikes

## Impact

Mousavi's death creates critical command-and-control vacuum in IRGC leadership:
1. No clear successor designated
2. IRGC operational capability disrupted during active conflict with US-Israel
3. Succession uncertainty may prevent coordinated military response
4. Iran's ability to enforce Strait of Hormuz closure questioned
